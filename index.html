shothorse


Shothorse is a simple app for manipulating screenshots.

Paste a screenshot into the window with ⌘+v and then hold a key and drag to mouse to draw shapes.

  • A and drag for arrows
  • L and drag for lines
  • B or R and drag for boxes (rectangles)

Pressing S will select the most recently-placed item, after which ...

  • Arrow keys will move the item
  • Holding Shift and dragging the mouse will move it
  • Holding Option or Alt and dragging the mouse will resize it
  • Backspace will delete the item

Pressing X will export the image back into your clipboard.

Officially on the web at shothorse.mcglashan.net
